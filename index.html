Slots & variants

All slots are optional. Composition order is fixed:

  1. label — Calm_Label_S, bright-green, uppercase (or mono numeral, top-right, in --full)
  2. head — icon (40×40 tile, 56×56 in --full, circular avatar in --testimonial) + title + optional subtitle
  3. body — supports HTML; italic in --testimonial
  4. cta — bright-green, underlined, arrow appended via ::after

Variants stack on the base .scard:

  • normal (default) — radius 16 · padding 24 · Calm_Title_M
  • --full — radius 24 · padding 32 28 · Calm_Title_L · hover lift · for feature pillars
  • --testimonial — radius 24 · circular avatar · italic body · Calm_Title_S
  • --inverse — for use over dark surfaces (combinable with above)
When a card has a cta and href, render as <a> (linked, hover state). Otherwise <div> (static).
